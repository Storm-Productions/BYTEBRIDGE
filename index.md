## Endpoints

### /Status
- **Description**: Retrieves the status of the system.
- **Method**: GET

### /Rules
- **Description**: Retrieves the rules of the system.
- **Method**: GET

### /DisabledProducts
- **Description**: Retrieves a list of disabled products.
- **Method**: GET

### /Resellers
- **Description**: Retrieves information about resellers.
- **Method**: GET
